DevIncept

- Learn by Developing

Tribute by Md Adnan Hussain

DevIncept is a company which is By Developers, For Developers. The concept is about open-source and building and growing the Community. I got to know about Devincept from LinkedIn when a connection of mine shared about it on his page. Curious to explore, I went to the LinkedIn page of DevIncept and then to the website. When reaching there i was very happy by seeing all the concepts of Free Learning and Open-Source. I was excited to get enrolled for the courses but the enrollment was closed. Since then I visited the page every 5-7 days to find if the Registration for next batch had begun and one fine day, I got the opportunity. I filled in the forms for 3 of the 8 courses which I was eager to learn, namely Python, Web Dev & DSA. Then days passed and on 2nd of July, I received mails confirming my seats in all the 3 courses. I was overjoyed after reading the mails and was full of enthusiasm and energy to learn new concepts and become a part of one big Open-Source Community.

Thank you DevIncept
